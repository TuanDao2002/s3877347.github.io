Project ideas

Information Technology is a fascinating industry and by joining it, we will be given opportunities to create and work with sophisticated software programs that has great applications in many areas such as business, healthcare, etc. However, there are many obstacles in software development that prevent us, which is why I have a great passion in Cloud Computing as it has many features that helps me to reduce the difficulties of creating new software programs, including the abilities to store information on cloud storage, host websites on cloud servers, create and deploy machine learning models on cloud platforms, etc. Thus, the growing process of transporting data and software to cloud environment is my expectation in the near future as it will not only affect the IT industry's progress in specific but also the Fourth Industrial Revolution in general. As “a survey by LogicMonitor concluded that AI will drive two-thirds of public cloud use by 2020” (Iron Mountain n.d.). So, a project that applies Cloud Computing and AI Machine Learning technologies will be a precious opportunity for me to sharpen my skills in exploiting cloud service and integrate cloud platforms with AI, help me to accumulate more knowledge on cloud platforms and hands-on experience with cloud tools supporting Machine Learning models. These are essential factors for me to be qualified for my ideal job as a cloud engineer.

Feedbacks

Here are some feedbacks on my project ideas from my team’s members:

Suk Jang Moon Le Ngoc Nguyen Thuan Nguyen Anh Duy

Nguyen Anh Duy

“Personally, I believe this application is a very meaningful project and an ethical way to help the disabled community. However, I would suggest you consider some of its drawbacks in order to improve its quality. You should think of fixing the mirror problem as when the camera is in front of the users, their right-hand side will be the left-hand side on the screen so it can make the application misunderstand the meaning of the gesture. Moreover, the applications may need to be improved to serve a wider range of users such as ones who lost one hand. Furthermore, a gesture may have different meanings in many sign languages, so the application must have the ability to identify all of them.”  

My discussion

The feedback from Duy is very informative and full of advices on how to improve the application to work better and produce greater experience for the users. I really agree with him about the advice on fixing the mirror problem as it can cause malfunction to the training model and affects the accuracy of the application. I also need to upgrade my training model to work with more kinds of disabled people including the handicapped and be able to understand many different meanings from a specific gesture. For example, the “OK” gesture in America means Okay but in Japan it means money (Bright Side, n.d.). His advice teaches me how to think of different hidden disadvantages meticulously and pay more attention to various types of users.  

SUK JANG MOON

“I believe it is a great idea since disabled people will have a better time talking to others more. Moreover, this project will help you practice more in your field and honed your skill more. I like the idea of making the app memory size small so that every phone can use it. If there is something I would improve is when recording the gesture and movement, they stored themselves on the phone so that the next time we used it, it will not take long to search the whole dictionary for that word.”  

My discussion

The feedback from Suk is useful and give me some creative ideas needed for my applications to function more smoothly. He not only shows me how this project will help me in the future but also advise me on how to optimize my application. I strongly agree on the idea of storing the recorded information on the device, it is such an innovative solution as it will significantly better my application’s performance and enhance the experience of the users. His advice is really helpful and remind me to always seek for optimization whenever I finished a project. With optimization, “Most savings and cost reductions can range from 15% to 20% of service costs in 18 to 36 months, and the highest savings can reach over 25%.” (Fabular, 2020).  

lE NGOC NGUYEN THUAN

“I particularly like this idea as it not only helps the disabled to communicate better but also help them to communicate in different sign languages in different areas in the world. It can help the users not to be cultural shock when moving to foreign countries. I think the application can be improved by working well in adverse conditions such as low – light environment, rainy or snowy weathers, etc.”  

My discussion

His feedback is informative and also outlines some unexpected benefits that I never think of before. He considers that this application will help many disabled people from many nations to communicate better and form a stronger community. I really agree with him about the idea of enhancing the applications to work in environments that will harm the accuracy and functionality of the application. A better AI model that can function in travers environments is possible, as researchers at Intel and the University of Illinois Urbana-Champlain created an AI that can see in low – light environment (Gershgorn, 2018). His advice instructs me to pay attention to the various conditions that will have a negative effect on the performance of the application and try to reduce all of the hindrances from external environments to make my application more competent.
